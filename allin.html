The project

ALL IN is a personal documentary film built as I live it. Not a vlog. Not a portfolio. A film — with a narrative, a structure, a musical thread. The soundtrack is Yumebanchi (夢番地), a song about dreams, time, and everything we carry without realizing it.

Why 8 parts?

Because my journey naturally breaks into 8 key moments. Each part = a video published on SHOJI (YouTube + Instagram). Parts unlock as I live them. What you see in grey hasn't happened yet — but it will.

Why ALL IN?

Because I put everything into this. McDonald's to save money. JLPT N3 in July. Working Holiday Visa. Leaving on July 10th, 2026. Keio SFC in 2027. No plan B.

"When you truly decide, there is no going back. There is only what you do next."
